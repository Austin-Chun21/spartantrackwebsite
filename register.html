How to Register

Follow the steps below to join the Spartan Track Club!

Step One: Register for USATF

USATF Membership is required to participate in USATF Hawaii Association track meets. Annual membership costs $25 for those 18 years and below, and costs $40 for adults 19 years and up. To register for USATF, click the link below
USATF Membership
Make sure to input the team code 39-0523 to be affiliated with the Spartan Track Club.

If you are a current member that needs to renew, you can look up your USATF membership number Here

Step Two: Register for Spartan Track Club
